Technology Philosophy and reCall Tools That Serve Humanity

Technology that Serves

We have a positive outlook on technology. We believe it is a wonderful tool that can be used to benefit every individual. We want to create tools that facilitate your life and relationships. reCall operates in the background, supporting real-world relationships rather than trying to become the center of users' social lives.

The Problem with Modern Technology

Our current information environment has been designed to capture attention, with devices acting as collections of services competing to show us ads. This environment often works against human flourishing rather than supporting it.

A Different Approach

At reCall, we believe technology should serve people, not the other way around. This philosophy manifests in several key principles:

Focus on Real Relationships

While many apps try to replace human connection with digital alternatives, reCall facilitates genuine interaction by helping people maintain their real-world relationships.

Respect for Attention

Unlike apps designed to be addictive, reCall is intentionally simple. It helps you stay connected with people who matter, then gets out of your way.

Privacy & Business Model

You are our customer, not our product. Our objective is to earn the money we make by producing valuable tools that you, the customer, are willing to pay for. The value proposition is very simple, we make you happy, you give us money. There is no back end business model exploiting your data. We keep your information private rather than mining it for advertising.

Looking Forward

This approach guides every decision as we develop reCall. We're demonstrating that technology can be both profitable and ethical, amplifying our humanity rather than diminishing it. It's about creating tools that enhance real human connection while respecting human dignity.
