In today's hyperconnected world, meaningful relationships often get lost in the digital noise. reCall is here to change that.

We help you build better relationships through intentional, consistent connection - without the distractions of social media.

What We Do

reCall is a thoughtfully designed mobile application that helps you maintain meaningful connections with the people who matter most in your life. Our app provides gentle reminders to reach out to friends and family at intervals you choose, helping you stay consistently present in their lives.

Building relationships isn't complicated, but it does require consistency. We're here to help you make it happen.

The Premise of reCall

Key Features

  • Customizable contact reminders for friends and family
  • Birthday and anniversary tracking
  • Personal notes to remember important details about loved ones
  • Clean, distraction-free interface focused on real connection

Our Philosophy

We believe in being social without the media. While technology has made it easier than ever to stay connected, it's also made it easier to substitute meaningful interaction with shallow engagement. reCall strips away the noise and focuses on what matters: genuine human connection.

Read More About Our Philosophy

How It Works

  1. Download the app
  2. Select the friends and family you want to stay in touch with
  3. Set how often you'd like to be reminded to reach out
  4. Receive gentle reminders to make those important connections
